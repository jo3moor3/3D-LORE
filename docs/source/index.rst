3D LORE
===================================

**3D LORE** is a wiki for 3D artists, riggers, technical animators, topology enthusiasts, video game developers, Motion graphics, VFX, and more.
I plan to post everything I learn here , and hope to keep growing it overtime. If eventully anyone cares to make additions, send me it at: **jo3moore@gmail.com** and I'll conider adding/changing it (with credits of course)


Check out the :doc:`Glossary` section for further information, including
fundamental vocab/terms for :ref:`Topology` , :ref:`Rigging` and :ref:`Animation`

.. note::

   This project is under active development.
